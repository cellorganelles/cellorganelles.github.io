About the CELL

A little franchise I made for fun. All these characters are based on the different organelles in the cell, plus enzymes and proteins. Together, they work in a facility called the Core Enterprise for Living Life (C.E.L.L.)

Hopefully through these silly characters, you'll memorize the organelles (especially if you're studying) and value the importance of the cell!

For more indepth descriptions of the CELL universe and the characters, check out the CELL info doc (aka "cell organelle funny").

You're free to use the characters for personal and educational purposes. This means using their illustrations directly as well as drawing them.

Examples include biology projects or presentations, fan art, etc. These are all acceptable. ^^

Please include credits if you can! (mention "Nunnerrs" or the website link)

However, do not use my artwork or characters for monetization or AI training. Please don't claim the characters as your own either. If you want to use them but are unsure if I'm okay with it, you can simply ask. (DM in Discord or create an issue)

Rane was slightly inspired by Sof's carbon atom character. Thanks to the organelle speed dating activity from AP Bio, the series was born, yay!

The CELL is not inspired by nor affiliated with Cells at Work! (to keep things clear)
